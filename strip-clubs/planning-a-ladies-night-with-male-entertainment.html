Common Planning Mistakes to Avoid

Don't Wait Until Last Minute

Quality performers book up quickly, especially for weekends. Plan at least 2-4 weeks ahead when possible. Last-minute bookings limit your options and may cost more.

Don't Skip the Research

Whether booking private performers or choosing venues, research thoroughly. Read reviews, check credentials, and verify legitimacy. Cutting corners here risks disappointing experiences.

Don't Assume Everyone's Comfortable

Discuss plans with your group beforehand rather than surprising them. Make sure everyone's genuinely on board with the entertainment type you've chosen.

Don't Forget About Logistics

Transportation, timing, food, drinks, and payment all require advance planning. Don't leave these details for the day of the event.

Don't Overspend

Set a realistic budget and stick to it. Entertainment should enhance your celebration, not create financial stress. There are options at every price point.
