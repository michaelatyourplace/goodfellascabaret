Group Size Considerations

Different entertainment options work better for different group sizes.

Small groups (4-8 people): Venue shows often make more sense economically. Private performances can feel awkward with very small groups, and per-person costs are higher. Consider male revue shows or combining with another activity.

Medium groups (8-15 people): Perfect size for private performances. Large enough for good energy and cost splitting, small enough to maintain intimacy. This size allows everyone to participate meaningfully.

Large groups (15-30 people): Ideal for private entertainment as costs split favorably and the larger audience creates great energy. May also work well for venue shows if you can coordinate transportation and seating.

Very large groups (30+ people): Venue shows typically work better for very large groups. Coordinating that many people at a private venue becomes challenging, and finding performers who handle very large private audiences can be difficult.
